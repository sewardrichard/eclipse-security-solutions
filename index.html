// SERVICE PORTFOLIO

Our Comprehensive
Service Portfolio

Eclipse Security Solutions offers an integrated suite of services designed to address every aspect of modern security risk.

01

Manned Guarding & Physical Security

  • Static Guarding: Uniformed, trained security officers deployed at fixed posts for 24/7 site security at commercial, industrial, and residential locations.
  • Armed & Unarmed Guards: Professionally vetted personnel, with armed units deployed based on rigorous risk assessment.
  • Access Control & Reception Services: Managing entry points, verifying credentials, and providing professional front-of-house security liaison.
  • Canine Unit Services: Trained dogs and handler teams for patrol, detection, and apprehension support.
02

Mobile Patrols & Rapid Armed Response

  • Scheduled & Randomised Patrols: GPS-tracked vehicle patrols to deter crime and inspect premises across residential areas, business parks, and industrial sites.
  • Armed Reaction Services: Immediate dispatch of trained response teams to alarm activations or emergency calls, 24 hours a day.
  • Key Holding & Alarm Response: Securely holding client keys and providing guaranteed response to alarm system triggers.
03

Cash & Valuables In-Transit (CVIT)

  • Secure Cash Transportation: Armoured vehicle logistics for banking, retail, and corporate cash movements.
  • Payroll & High-Value Item Delivery: Secure transport of payroll, sensitive documents, pharmaceuticals, and other valuables.
  • ATM Replenishment & Servicing: Full secure logistics support for financial institutions.
04

Electronic Security & Technology Integration

  • Alarm System Installation & Monitoring: Sales, installation, and 24/7 monitoring of intrusion detection systems.
  • CCTV Surveillance Systems: Supply, installation, and monitoring of analog and IP-based video surveillance with remote viewing capabilities.
  • Access Control Systems: Implementation of electronic entry systems (biometric, card, pin) for enhanced perimeter security.
  • Perimeter Protection: Electric fencing, seismic sensors, and beam detection systems.
05

Specialised Security & Risk Consulting

  • Security Risk Assessments & Audits: Comprehensive site vulnerability analyses and detailed recommendations for physical and procedural security enhancement.
  • Event Security Management: Crowd control, access management, and VIP protection for corporate functions, festivals, and public events.
  • Executive & Close Protection: Discreet, professional protection services for executives, high-net-worth individuals, and visiting dignitaries.
  • Security Training Programs: Customised training for client staff on emergency response, fraud prevention, and security awareness.
  • Security Guard Training: Comprehensive basic and advanced training programs for security personnel, covering legal frameworks, customer service, emergency procedures, report writing, and professional conduct.
06

Remote Monitoring & Control Room Services

  • Centralised 24/7 Control Room: State-of-the-art facility monitoring all security systems, coordinating patrols, and managing incident response.
  • Remote Video Monitoring & Guard Tour Verification: Proactive surveillance of CCTV feeds and verification of guard patrols via smartphone technology.
  • Client Portal Access: Real-time online access to incident reports, patrol logs, and system status for complete transparency.
07

Cybersecurity Liaison & Physical-Digital Convergence

  • Physical Security for IT Infrastructure: Securing server rooms and data centres.
  • Consultation on Converged Security: Advising on the integration of physical security systems with IT networks to prevent emerging hybrid threats.
